\subsection{ORB-SLAM3}
\label{sec:orb_slam3}

ORB-SLAM3 is a KV-SLAM implementation that is popular in research contexts due to its performance, open-source codebase, and use of methods and techniques that are well documented and widely understood. Figure \ref{fig:orb-slam3} shows a simplified diagram of the internal processes of ORB-SLAM3. The system supports a variety of input modalities including monocular and stereo cameras, RGB-D cameras, and IMU data. It splits work across multiple threads, allowing it to perform tracking, mapping, loop closure, relocalization, and optimization concurrently.

\subsubsection{Map Points and Native Culling}
\label{sec:orb_slam3_map_points}

ORB-SLAM3 represents the environment as a set of 3D map points, each associated with a visual descriptor and a history of keyframe observations, and applies native culling rules to remove points that fail quality checks during local mapping.
