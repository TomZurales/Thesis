\subsection{Road Map}

Chapter \ref{sec:background} provides a high-level overview of the concepts core to this research, including the inner workings of KV-SLAM, relocalization, RANSAC, and map point culling.

Chapter \ref{sec:related_work} digs deeper into research related to improving long-term SLAM operations and detecting and removing extraneous data from SLAM maps.

Chapter \ref{sec:implementation} discusses the implementation details of a viewpoint-based historical observability model, the creation of a point removal system that implements this model, and its integration into ORB-SLAM3. Additionally, the hardware and software supporting the collection of the stereo visual datasets are described.

Chapter \ref{sec:analysis} covers the tests performed on the observability model, the existence probability estimator, and the integrated SLAM system. Tests are performed in simulation to establish initial parameters and on generated datasets to compare real-world and simulated performance.

Chapter \ref{sec:conclusion} aggregates the results from Chapter \ref{sec:analysis} and evaluates them against the defined objectives of this research to determine whether this method is an effective solution to the problems discussed so far. Areas requiring further study are also discussed.
