\subsection{ORB-SLAM3}
\label{sec:orb_slam3}

ORB-SLAM3 is a KV-SLAM implementation popular in research contexts due to its performance, open-source codebase, and use of well-documented, widely understood techniques. Figure \ref{fig:orb-slam3} shows a simplified diagram of ORB-SLAM3’s internal processes. ORB-SLAM3 supports a variety of input modalities including monocular and stereo cameras, RGB-D cameras, and IMU data. It splits work across multiple threads, allowing it to perform tracking, local mapping, loop closing, relocalization, and optimization concurrently.
